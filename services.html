Rapid Response Run Cards

Rapid Response is an application designed to make the dispatch of critcal resources more effective. Keep all the resources dispatchers need to stay organized in one location. Rapid Response will also time the dispatcher and give audio and visual reminders of how long it has been since the situation has begun.
